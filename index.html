Common Math Techniques

Algebra

Algebra is the study of mathematical symbols and the rules for manipulating these symbols. It's the foundation for more advanced topics in math.

Calculus

Calculus is the study of change and motion, and it helps to describe dynamic systems. It has two main branches: differential and integral calculus.

Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics concerning the properties and relationships of points, lines, surfaces, and solids.

Trigonometry

Trigonometry deals with the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles. It's essential in many fields like physics and engineering.

Linear Algebra

Linear algebra is the study of vectors, vector spaces, and linear equations. It's widely used in physics, computer science, and economics.

Statistics

Statistics involves the collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization of data.

Number Theory

Number theory is the study of integers and the relationships between them. It's often called "higher arithmetic" because of its deep and rich history.
